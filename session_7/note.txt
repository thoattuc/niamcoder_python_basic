* Thuoc tinh - Atributes

* Phuong thuc - Method
    xac dinh cac hanh vi, chuc nang ma cac doi tuong lop co
    Ham != Phuong thuc:
        Ham nam ben ngoai va khong lien quan den class, goi 1 cach doc lap;
        Phuong thuc nam ben trong lop, goi thong qua lop va doi tuong

* self:
    tham so dau tien cua tat ca cac ham cua lop
    self.param truy cap hoac thay doi gia tri cac thuoc tinh.

* Khoi tao doi tuong:
    instance_name = Class_name(params) - khong can truyen vao doi so self
    del instance_name

* Su dung phuong thuc cua lop:
    instance_name.method(args)

* Truy cap thuoc tinh cua doi tuong:
    instance_name.attribute
    instance_name.attribute = value
    del instance_name.attribute
